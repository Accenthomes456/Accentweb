Who We Are

Founded in 2009, Climaire Mechanical Inc. began with one mission: to deliver heating and cooling solutions that genuinely improve people’s lives. We’re not just HVAC professionals — we’re neighbors, parents, homeowners, and small business owners just like you. We understand the importance of coming home to a space that’s warm in the winter, cool in the summer, and clean all year round.

As a family-owned and locally operated company, we’ve spent over a decade serving the diverse climates of Southwest Colorado and New Mexico. Whether you're facing a high-desert heatwave or a Rocky Mountain snowstorm, we bring the expertise and reliability your home or business needs.

What Sets Us Apart

While many HVAC companies chase volume, we focus on value. Every home is different, every client unique — and that’s exactly how we treat every job.

We are proud to be an authorized Carrier Dealer, offering top-tier equipment backed by proven performance. But more importantly, we bring integrity, clear communication, and problem-solving to every service call. From simple tune-ups to full HVAC system redesigns, we do it right the first time.

We believe in long-term relationships, not one-time fixes.
